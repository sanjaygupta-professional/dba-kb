# DBA Knowledge Base — Overview

This vault is a compiled knowledge base for the **DBA in AI programme** delivered by upGrad-INSOFE in partnership with Golden Gate University, San Francisco. It distils 246 ingested sources — lecture transcripts, slide decks, course outlines — from Courses 03 through 07 into 257 interconnected reference pages. Built using the Karpathy LLM-wiki pattern: raw source material is compiled once into structured wiki pages, then queried and updated as knowledge grows.

## Major Themes

- **AI Foundations** — Supervised and unsupervised machine learning: [[linear-regression]], [[logistic-regression]], [[clustering]], [[decision-trees]], [[ensemble-methods]], [[association-rule-mining]]. Covers the mathematical and conceptual toolkit from Course 04.
- **Deep Learning** — Neural architecture, training dynamics, and specialised networks (CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, Transformers): [[deep-learning]], [[transfer-learning]]. Built from 15 sessions of Course 05 with Dr. Srinivasa Varadharajan.
- **Generative AI** — Pre-trained models, LLMs, fine-tuning, RLHF, multimodal systems, and enterprise deployment patterns. Derived from 14 sessions of Course 06.
- **Emerging Digital Technologies** — IoT, quantum computing, neuromorphic systems, AR/VR/XR, digital twins, RPA, blockchain, and cybersecurity: [[emerging-digital-technologies]]. Course 03's 15-session survey by external industry practitioners.
- **AI Project Strategy & DBA Research** — How to move from AI enthusiasm to structured strategy; [[ai-project-strategy]] (AI Crush vs AI Strategy framework), [[doctoral-research-methodology]] (thesis modes, DBA vs PhD scope, golden dataset strategy). From Course 07 with [[prof-dakshina-morti]].

# Emerging Digital Technologies

The course category framing used in [[course-03-overview-emerging-digital-technologies|Course_03]]. Refers to the technology set that **complements rather than replaces AI** in industry use cases.

## Scope (as surfaced in Session 01)

- **IoT** — data acquisition at the edge, the subject of the next-day lecture.
- **AR/VR** — customer-experience applications; cited as a category worth DBA research attention.
- **RPA** — robotic process automation combined with agentic AI for compliance monitoring.
- **Edge AI** — on-device inference, bridging IoT and ML.
- **Blockchain** — referenced implicitly as part of the digital-tech mix ^[inferred].

## Why the bundling matters

Dr. Sumitra Padman's framing in [[course-03-session-01-transcript|the kickoff]]: cohort feedback showed AI alone was insufficient for DBA candidates coming from non-ML industries. Pairing AI with these adjacent technologies gives each candidate an application surface that matches their industry.

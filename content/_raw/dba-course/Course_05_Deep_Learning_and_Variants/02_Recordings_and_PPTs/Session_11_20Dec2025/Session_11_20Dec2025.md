## Key Takeaways

**Topic:** Convolutional neural networks — architecture, filters, pooling, and milestones

1. **ImageNet Dataset** `[25:35]` — 14M images across 20k+ categories.
2. **Convolutional Neural Networks** `[28:09]` — Hierarchical conv-based feature extractors.
3. **Neocognitron** `[44:19]` — Early biologically-inspired CNN.
4. **Feature Learning** `[48:05]` — Auto-learned features vs hand-crafting.
5. **Convolution Operation** `[49:37]` — Filter × patch → sum.
6. **Feature Extraction** `[50:29]` — Detect edges, orientations, patterns.
7. **Stride** `[54:25]` — Pixel step when sliding the filter.
8. **Feature Maps** `[56:09]` — Conv-layer output matrices.
9. **Sparse Connectivity** `[66:04]` — Local only — huge parameter savings vs dense.
10. **Neuron as Filter** `[61:04]` — A CNN neuron *is* a filter with fixed weights.
11. **Hyperparameters** `[69:30]` — Filter size, count, stride, pooling.
12. **Multiple Filters** `[72:55]` — Diverse features per layer.
13. **Pooling Layers** `[83:22]` — Spatial downsampling.
14. **Max Pooling** `[85:10]` — Take the max — preserves strongest activations.
15. **Padding** `[95:28]` — Border pixels preserve spatial dims.
16. **Flattening** `[99:09]` — 3D maps → 1D vectors for dense layers.
17. **Fully Connected Layers** `[154:01]` — Final dense classifier.
18. **ReLU Activation** `[153:35]` — Hidden-layer activation replacing sigmoid.
19. **AlexNet Architecture** `[154:22]` — 8-layer breakthrough with 5×5 filters, max-pool, ReLU, augmentation.
20. **Batch Normalisation & Dropout** `[153:45]` — Regularisation tandem.

## Key Takeaways

**Topic:** Deep neural network training, challenges, and regularisation

1. **Neuron Model** `[06:09]` — Inputs, weights, bias, transfer, activation.
2. **Bias** `[06:45]` — Background activity signal *b*.
3. **Weight** `[07:01]` — Learnable multiplier on features.
4. **Transfer Function** `[07:27]` — Linear combination pre-activation.
5. **Activation Function** `[07:44]` — Nonlinear output shaping.
6. **Sigmoid Activation** `[07:54]` — Logistic — binary classification.
7. **Identity Activation** `[08:13]` — Linear — regression.
8. **Universal Approximation** `[10:13]` — Enough neurons in one layer ≈ any function.
9. **Overfitting (single wide layer)** `[10:53]` — Risk of too many neurons in one layer.
10. **Multi-layer Networks** `[11:11]` — Two layers beat one wide layer for nonlinearity.
11. **Backpropagation** `[12:23]` — Error propagated backward to update weights.
12. **Gradient Descent** `[13:07]` — Opposite-gradient step × LR.
13. **Learning Rate** `[13:33]` — Controls update magnitude.
14. **Online Updating** `[15:43]` — Per-sample updates.
15. **Batch Updating** `[18:23]` — Whole-set updates.
16. **Mini-Batch Updating** `[19:20]` — Small-chunk sweet spot.
17. **Epoch** `[16:47]` — One full dataset pass.
18. **Convergence** `[16:20]` — Gradient ≈ 0 or tolerance hit.
19. **Deep Neural Networks** `[21:27]` — >2 hidden layers for complex problems.
20. **Image Flattening** `[26:52]` — 2D image → 1D vector for DNN input.
21. **Dropout** `[169:00]` — Stochastic connection removal for regularisation.
22. **Regularisation** `[171:17]` — Move away from overfitting toward generalisation.

## Key Takeaways

**Topic:** Neural networks, deep learning, and artificial intelligence fundamentals

1. **Data Science** `[64:01]` — Statistics + probability + calculus + programming + domain; the alphabet of AI.
2. **Machine Learning** `[65:00]` — Mathematical input→output models learned from data, not hand-coded.
3. **Supervised Learning** `[92:12]` — Labels given; parameters adjusted via accuracy feedback.
4. **Unsupervised Learning** `[99:42]` — No labels; system finds clusters via similarity metric.
5. **Deep Learning** `[68:18]` — ML via neuron-modelled networks.
6. **Artificial Intelligence** `[69:07]` — Broader than ML; includes agentic systems.
7. **Synapse** `[126:44]` — Chemical junction; strength = learned weight.
8. **Neurotransmitters** `[129:27]` — Chemical messengers released at synapses.
9. **Threshold** `[132:50]` — Input + background must exceed it to fire.
10. **Hebbian Learning** `[144:38]` — "Neurons that fire together wire together."
11. **Perceptron** `[160:01]` — Math model of a single neuron.
12. **Weights** `[157:02]` — Learnable synaptic strengths.
13. **Bias** `[161:48]` — Background activity term.
14. **Activation Function** `[158:36]` — Sigmoid for classification, linear for regression.
15. **Single Layer Neural Network** `[170:22]` — Input → one hidden layer → output.
16. **Universal Approximation Theorem** `[168:49]` — Any classifier achievable with enough neurons in one hidden layer.
17. **Hidden Layer** `[170:21]` — Intermediate layer of neurons.
18. **Dense Neural Network** `[174:01]` — Every-to-every connection.
19. **Feed Forward Neural Network** `[176:09]` — Information flows forward only.
20. **Deep Neural Network** `[171:16]` — More than two hidden layers — the locus of deep learning.

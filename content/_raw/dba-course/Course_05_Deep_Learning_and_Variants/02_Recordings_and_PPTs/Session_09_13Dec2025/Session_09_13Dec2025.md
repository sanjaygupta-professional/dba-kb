**Topic:** Building a CNN from scratch + transfer learning for brain-tumour classification

1. **Google Colab** `[38:15]` — Free cloud IDE with CPU/GPU/TPU.
2. **Image Preprocessing** `[126:08]` — Resize + array conversion.
3. **One-Hot Encoding** `[126:18]` — Categorical labels → binary form.
4. **Sequential Model** `[126:49]` — Keras layer-by-layer API.
5. **Convolutional Layer 2D** `[127:11]` — Filter-based spatial feature extraction (32/64/128).
6. **Activation Function (ReLU)** `[130:16]` — Non-linearity for complex patterns.
7. **Batch Normalisation** `[131:11]` — Stabilises training.
8. **Max Pooling** `[131:15]` — Local-region max downsampling.
9. **Flatten Layer** `[130:16]` — 2D maps → 1D vector.
10. **Dense Layer** `[130:08]` — Fully connected head.
11. **Dropout** `[131:27]` — Stochastic deactivation for regularisation.
12. **Sigmoid Activation** `[131:49]` — Binary classification output.
13. **Adam Optimizer** `[132:13]` — Adaptive weight-update algorithm.
14. **Cross-Entropy Loss** `[132:20]` — Classification loss.
15. **Epoch** `[132:56]` — Full training pass.
16. **Batch Size** `[133:02]` — Samples per update.
17. **Validation Split** `[133:06]` — 80/20 train/val typical.
18. **Overfitting** `[134:35]` — High train acc, low val acc.
19. **Transfer Learning** `[149:34]` — Pre-trained weights cut compute and boost small-data perf.
20. **Fine-tuning** `[191:30]` — Retrain outer layers; keep extractors.

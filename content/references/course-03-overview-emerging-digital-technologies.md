# Course 03 — Emerging Digital Technologies

A newly-launched course in the DBA Emerging Technologies GenAI program at [[upgrad|upGrad]], anchored by [[dr-sumitra-padman|Dr. Sumitra Padman]]. Designed to bridge AI with neighbouring technology domains — IoT, AR/VR, RPA, blockchain — so DBA candidates from diverse industries can apply AI to real business contexts.

## Structure

15 sessions, heavy peer-learning component, guest lectures by industry experts. See [[concepts/emerging-digital-technologies|Emerging Digital Technologies]] for the domain scope.

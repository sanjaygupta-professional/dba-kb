# Prof. Dakshina Morti V Kuru

**Also known as**: Professor Morty, Morti  
**Role**: Instructor, [[course-07-overview-ai-project-design-and-execution|Course 07 — AI Project Design and Execution]]  
**Programme**: upGrad-INSOFE DBA in AI

## Background

PhD in Material Science and Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University. One of the lead scientists on India's Agni long-range ballistic missile programme. Pivoted to AI/analytics in 2000 after choosing laptops over furnaces as the accessible entrepreneur's tool. Self-described "seed entrepreneur" — has built and sold multiple small companies; prefers education and hacking over consulting. Has guided 10+ DBA students to graduation.

## Current Work (as of early 2026)

Building **RISI** — Regulated Inexpensive Specialized Intelligence. Small groups of specialised LLMs that outperform larger general-purpose models in regulated domains (e.g., regulatory affairs in pharma). Strategy: build a gold-standard Q&A benchmark for a domain → demonstrate a small model beats GPT-4/Gemini on that benchmark.

Training and advisory arm of his company was being acquired (April 15, 2026). Fairly active on LinkedIn.

## Key Frameworks Introduced in Course 07

- **[[concepts/ai-project-strategy|AI Crush vs AI Strategy]]** — the distinction between vague AI excitement and structured strategic planning
- **3-Layer AI Planning Framework**: Strategy → Decision → Workflow
- **DBA Thesis Selection Framework**: 3 output modes (paper/book/product), golden dataset strategy, DBA vs PhD scope distinction

# upGrad

EdTech company delivering the DBA program captured in this wiki, in partnership with Golden Gate University (which accredits the doctoral degree).

## Key facts

- **Acquired [[insofe|INSOFE]]** — bringing its AI-academic faculty (including [[dr-sridhar-pappu|Dr. Sridhar Pappu]]) into the upGrad fold.
- **DBA tech programs** led academically by [[dr-sumitra-padman|Dr. Sumitra Padman]].
- **Learning portal**: learn.upgrad.com — hosts session recordings, reading material, Teams forums for cohort discussion.

## Role in the program

- Curates cohorts of diverse business backgrounds.
- Arranges industry experts as guest instructors per session.
- Handles logistics for 3-year DBA delivery.

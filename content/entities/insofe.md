# INSOFE

International School of Engineering — a Hyderabad-based institution focused on AI and data-science education. Co-founded by [[dr-sridhar-pappu|Dr. Sridhar Pappu]] (who served as Executive VP-Academics and Professor). Acquired by [[upgrad|upGrad]] and now forms part of its advanced-education portfolio.

## Role in this DBA program

INSOFE's faculty lineage supplies several of the early Course_04 (ML & AI Foundations) instructors — [[dr-sridhar-pappu|Dr. Pappu]] being the most visible in Session 01.

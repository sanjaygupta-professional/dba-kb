# Dr. Srinivasa Varadharajan Lakshminarasimhan (Rajan)

PhD in Physics (2002, University of Missouri – St. Louis), MSc Physics (IIT Madras, 1993). Deep Learning and Variants (Course 05) instructor in the upGrad DBA program.

## Roles

- **Director and Academic Head**, Artiq Insights Pvt Ltd, Chennai (current)
- **Professor of the Practice**, Golden Gate University, San Francisco (current)
- **Professor and Dean (R&D)**, INSOFE / upGrad-INSOFE, Hyderabad (Feb 2019 – Nov 2023)
- Research Scientist, L V Prasad Eye Institute, Hyderabad (2013–2019)
- Associate Professor / Assistant Professor, Sankara Nethralaya, Chennai (2004–2012)
- Postdoctoral Researcher, UC Santa Barbara (2001–2004)

## Research Background

Background in computational physics and optics, pivoting to ML/DL applications in medical imaging and data science education. Author of *Understanding Optics with Python* (Taylor & Francis, 2016) and *Special Functions for Optical Science and Engineering* (SPIE, 2015). 25+ research publications.
